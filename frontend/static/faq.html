Frequently Asked Questions

How to order food? + -

After you have selected your city, you can choose a restaurant from the list. Then, select the food you want to order and add it to your cart. Finally, proceed to checkout and enter your delivery details.

How to register? + -

Just click on the "Register" button on the top right corner of the page. Fill in your details and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

How to become a delivery driver? + -

To become a delivery driver, you need to fill out an application form on our website. You can find the form in your account settings after logging in.

Can I schedule a delivery in advance? + -

Yes, during checkout, you can choose a preferred delivery time and date for eligible restaurants.

How can I track my order? + -

After placing your order, you'll receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can also check order status in your account dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept? + -

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and sometimes cash, depending on the restaurant.
